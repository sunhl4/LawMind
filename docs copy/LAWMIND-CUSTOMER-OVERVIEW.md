# LawMind customer overview (one page)

**LawMind** is a legal workbench for structured tasks, research-backed drafts, human review, and Office exports. It builds on OpenClaw-era patterns with a **local-first** workspace.

## Capabilities (high level)

- Task and draft lifecycle with **auditability**
- Assistant personas and optional **multi-assistant collaboration** (in-product)
- Word and presentation export paths (see [user manual](/LAWMIND-USER-MANUAL))

## Data residency

- Primary data stays in the **customer-controlled workspace** on disk. Hosted **model providers** remain sub-processors chosen by the customer; see [LawMind data processing](/LAWMIND-DATA-PROCESSING).

## Support

- Support channels and SLAs are defined in your **order form** or **MSA** (placeholders here). Technical references: [LawMind support runbook](/LAWMIND-SUPPORT-RUNBOOK) (internal), [LawMind delivery](/LAWMIND-DELIVERY) (customer-facing).

## Boundaries

- **Not legal advice**: outputs require professional review before filing or client delivery.
- **Integrations**: no built-in DMS or billing; see [LawMind integrations](/LAWMIND-INTEGRATIONS).
